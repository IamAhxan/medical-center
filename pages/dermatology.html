Dermatology Services

MORPHEUS 8

Morpheus8 is a skin rejuvenation treatment. It combines microneedling with radiofrequency (RF) energy. The tiny needles deliver RF energy deep into your skin, triggering the production of new collagen and elastin, creating firmer skin.

ACTION II Fractional Laser- Action II Petite Lady

This fractional laser uses multiple stacking technology to safely double the depth of penetration and speed up tissues regeneration. Action II helps to treat scars, wrinkles, uneven pigmentation, while also helps to tighten skin and reduce pores. Leaving your skin with a shinier new look. Action II Petite Lady can penetrate the vaginal wall to generate new collagen to tighten the vaginal wall.

ULTRAFORMER

Is a new technology that uses heat generated from high intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU) waves to create coagulation zones deep in the skin. Coagulation causes immediate lifting impression and triggers a collagen regenration process, the result is noninvasive lifting of loose skin of the face and neck without any surgery.

SPECTRA CARBON

This carbon laser is used to remove skin pigments, both deep and on the surface. Skin rejuvenation is also achieved through carbon peeling on Spectra mode, by heating the skin after carbon lotion is absorbed for deep peeling to stimulate new collagen. Spectra can also be used for fine hair removal, hair bleaching, pore reduction, skin tightening, improved texture, acne and tattoos.

LASEMD

Provides the skin with a youthful glow, evens out pigmentation, while also improving the skins texture and laxity. This laser treatment utilizes nanotechnology that heats up deeper layers of the skin without harming the surface to produce new collagen. LaseMD also utilizes a patented cream that forms multiple barriers on the skin hydrated and keeps nanoparticle-based nutrients contained.

GentleMax Pro Laser Hair Removals

This laser hair removal has dynamic cooling device technology for optimal comfort and safety during treatments, while providing the fastest completion for full body sessions. GentleMax Pro also delivers the best hair removal technology as the laser wavelengths has a higher absorption rate on melanin, where the energy thermally damages targeted hair bulbs to prevents future regrowth. Reduction in hair is noticeable from the first session.

Cleopatra

Fraxel laser treatment targets aging and sun-damaged skin with microscopic laser columns that penetrate deep into your skin to expedite your body’s remodeling of collagen. Since the laser treats only a fraction of tissue at a time, it leaves the surrounding tissue intact, which promotes very rapid healing. It resurfaces your skin by stimulating the growth of new, healthy skin cells from the inside out.

INFINI

Uses fractional mirco-needling radio frequency technology, which combines needles and heating effects to help the body produce new collagen. This in turn reduces acne scars, pores, and stretch marks, while also tightens skin and improves its texture.

FILLER

A syringe containing hyaluronic acid. Dermal fillers can be injected into different parts of the face and neck, in order to volumize, contour, enhance, and reshape various features. Patients can achieve desirable results such as eyebrow shaping/lifting, reduced appearance of under eye bags, as well as enhanced lips, cheeks, and chin with strategic placements for added volume. Non-surgical facelifts and nose reshaping can also be achieved with dermal fillers.

SILKPEEL

SilkPeel™️ delivers noticeable results, immediately. Unlike microdermabrasion or chemical peels, SilkPeel™️ Dermalinfusion’s diamond-tip application system is non-invasive, free of aluminum crystals and leaves the skin feeling fresh and radiant, not irritated. The SilkPeel™️ procedure is safe, painless and can be performed almost twice as frequently as microdermabrasion without the risk of aggravating sensitive skin.
